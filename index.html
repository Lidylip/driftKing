About Drift King

Drift King is an exhilarating online racing game that puts you behind the wheel of high-performance drift cars. Experience the perfect blend of realistic physics and arcade-style gameplay as you master the art of drifting through challenging courses and compete for the highest scores.

Game Features

Realistic Physics

Experience authentic drifting mechanics with precise car control and realistic physics simulation.

Multiple Cars

Choose from a variety of drift-tuned vehicles, each with unique handling characteristics.

Challenging Tracks

Test your skills on diverse tracks designed to push your drifting abilities to the limit.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to control your car
  • Hold spacebar to initiate drifts
  • Chain drifts together for higher scores
  • Complete challenges to unlock new cars and tracks
